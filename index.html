VETHATHIRI MAHARHISHI

SPIRTUAL LEADER

Shri Vethathiri Maharishi (1911–2006) was a spiritual leader and founder-trustee of “The World Community Service Centre”.
From his youth he was driven by the desire for knowledge and in particular strove to find answers to three questions:
  • What is God?
  • What is life?
  • Why is poverty in the world?
